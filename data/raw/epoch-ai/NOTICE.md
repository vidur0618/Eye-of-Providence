# Epoch AI data snapshots

Snapshots in this directory are downloaded from [Epoch AI's AI Data Centers dataset](https://epoch.ai/data/data-centers-documentation).

Epoch states that the data may be used, distributed, and reproduced with source and author credit under the Creative Commons Attribution license. Retain the generated manifest and this notice with redistributed snapshots.

Each dated directory is immutable after publication. A correction or upstream refresh receives a new access-date directory. `scripts/fetch-epoch.mjs` refuses to overwrite a snapshot unless the operator explicitly passes `--force`; force is for pre-publication recovery, not historical revision.
